About us

                
Welcome to Variant, a proudly Egyptian clothing brand that offers a unique blend of traditional and modern fashion. Our company was founded in [2021] with a mission to provide high-quality, locally-made clothing to our valued customers.
                    Our designs are inspired by the rich cultural heritage of Egypt, while also incorporating modern fashion trends to create something truly special.
                    At Variant, we believe in creating sustainable fashion that is both stylish and environmentally-friendly. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and minimizing waste in every aspect of our business, from production to packaging.
                    Thank you for choosing Variant for your fashion needs. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy creating them
